About Us

OUR GOAL AND VISION

The goal of BookMyMovie is to reach every movie theatre in your city. As the number one ticketing platform in India, its objective is to ensure that you receive the finest discounts and deals on ticket purchases.

BookMyMovie started the Revolution in Movie Booking sites. And we went on to become India’s leading movie booking website. Today, more than 5 Million users are powered by BookMyMovie to get tickets digitally. This is because more than 3 million Indians use BookMyMovie to book movie ticket at their homes. And that’s not all, BookMyMovies App is used to Pay less, do Recharges, Send money to friends & family, Book movies & travel tickets. BookMyShow started out in 1999 as a software re-seller for movie theaters and converted into a platform catering to cloud-based ticket booking of events, movies, sports, and plays. BookMyMovies was known by the name of its parent company, Bizztree Entertainment Pvt. Ltd., at the time of its inception.
